SHRRADHA HIGH SCHOOL GUIDELINES & INFORMATION

1. About the School
Shrradha High School is a private educational institution located in Reddy Colony, P.R. Pally, Sangareddy. We follow the Telangana State Board syllabus and provide quality education from Nursery to 10th standard. The medium of instruction is English, with Telugu and Hindi as additional languages. We focus on discipline, academic growth, and character development.

2. Admissions
Admissions are open for all classes from Nursery to 10th standard, subject to seat availability. 
Requirements: Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card, and previous academic records. 
Process: Parents can apply by visiting the school office and filling out the admission form.

3. Timings & Schedule
School Timings: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday to Saturday.
Holidays: Second Saturday is generally a half-day. Sundays are holidays.

4. Fees & Payments
Fees are affordable and can be paid in installments. Payment methods include cash and UPI. For exact fee structures, parents must contact the school office.

5. Facilities
- Well-ventilated classrooms
- Library with academic and general knowledge books
- Basic science lab facilities
- Computer education from early classes
- Open space for outdoor activities like cricket and kabaddi
- Clean drinking water and sanitation facilities

6. Transport
Transport facilities are available for students in nearby areas. Safety is a priority. Parents can request transport details at the school office.

7. Academics
We conduct regular weekly tests, monthly assessments, and term examinations. Special attention and revision sessions are provided for 10th standard board exams.

8. Rules & Discipline
Uniform is compulsory. Mobile phones are strictly prohibited on campus. Students must maintain respectful behavior.
